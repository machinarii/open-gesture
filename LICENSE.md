## Research Attribution

The metadata schema in this dataset is grounded in peer-reviewed research.
If you use this dataset in academic work, published applications, or
derivative datasets, you are encouraged to cite the foundational works that
informed its design. At a minimum, please acknowledge:

1. Noroozi, F., Corneanu, C. A., Kaminska, D., Sapinski, T., Escalera, S., & Anbarjafari, G. (2018). Survey on emotional body gesture recognition. *IEEE Transactions on Affective Computing*.
2. Lhommet, M., & Marsella, S. (2014). Expressing emotion through posture and gesture. In *The Oxford Handbook of Affective Computing*. Oxford University Press.
3. Yang, Z., & Narayanan, S. (2016). Modeling dynamics of expressive body gestures in dyadic interactions. *IEEE Transactions on Affective Computing*. DOI: 10.1109/TAFFC.2016.2542812.
4. de Byl, P. (2015). A conceptual affective design framework for the use of emotions in computer game design. *Cyberpsychology*, 9(3), Article 4. DOI: 10.5817/CP2015-3-4.
5. Wachs, J., Stern, H., & Edan, Y. (2008). A holistic framework for hand gestures design. Naval Postgraduate School & Ben-Gurion University of the Negev.
6. Nagy, R., Kucherenko, T., Moell, B., Pereira, A., Kjellstrom, H., & Bernardet, U. (2021). A framework for integrating gesture generation models into interactive conversational agents. In *Proc. AAMAS 2021*.
7. Jessop, E. N. (2010). *A Gestural Media Framework*. Master's thesis, MIT.

And the foundational theoretical works referenced in the schema:

- Ekman, P., & Friesen, W. V. (1969). The repertoire of nonverbal behavior. *Semiotica*, 1(1), 49-98.
- McNeill, D. (1992). *Hand and Mind: What Gestures Reveal about Thought*. University of Chicago Press.
- Plutchik, R. (1980). *Emotion: A Psychoevolutionary Synthesis*. Harper & Row.
- Russell, J. A., & Mehrabian, A. (1977). Evidence for a three-factor theory of emotions. *Journal of Research in Personality*, 11(3), 273-294.
- Dael, N., Mortillaro, M., & Scherer, K. R. (2012). The Body Action and Posture coding system (BAP). *Journal of Nonverbal Behavior*, 36(2), 97-132.

A complete reference list is available in the [README](README.md#research-references).
